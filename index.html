about me

Elena graduated from Syracuse University with a BFA in Illustration in 2020, during the height of the CO-VID 19 Pandemic. Reflecting on her career during quarantine, she decided to venture into the world of UX/UI Design.
Getting her certificate from Northwestern's UX/UI Bootcamp in August 2022, she hopes to bring her unique style and personality to the world of Interface Design.
